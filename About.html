Our Drilling Facilities

We have drilling facilities located across the globe pumping about 3000 gallons of crude oil daily, we have drilling platforms located in the UAE, in LUXEMBOURG and in CHINA, they are all located on the ocean staying far away as possible from residents and also taking adequate measures in pumping the crude oil

Our Refinery

Crude oil pumped from the ocean are taken to the refinery for further processing, they are stored in distillation tubes and heated at a high temperature , they are provided with different chambers with differrent products

Distribution of finished products

Different products like kerosene, petrol, gas, diesel, are stored in different barrels to be delivered to filling stations, private enties, companys and exported to other countries
